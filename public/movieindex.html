Best Movies According to Moeed

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Uncharted

Great action and adventure movie. The story is based on a game and is about finding the treasure that was left by Ferdinand Magellan when he was doing the first circumnavigation of the world.

Dune

The visuals in combination with the sound/music are hauntingly beautiful and will stick in your mind for a long time. The movie is slow paced, so if you are accustomed to constant dopamine stimulation than you might find it boring.

Dungeons & Dragons:Honor Among Thieves

No need for prior knowledge of anything about D&D. Extremely funny especially the reviving the dead skeleton scene. A cross between Indiana Jones & Lord of the Rings.
